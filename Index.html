An Introduction to Web development

Web development is the process of building websites and web applications that you access through a browser (like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari).

Why semantics matter

semantics matter in HTML because they give meaning to the content of a webpage, both for machines (like browsers and screen readers) and humans (like developers and users).
